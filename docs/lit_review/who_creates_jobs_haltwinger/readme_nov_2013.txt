To access the data for the paper "Who Creates Jobs?  Small vs. Large vs. Young" a researcher must
apply to the U.S. Census Bureau.  The Center for Economic Studies operates 15 Research Data Centers (RDC) around the
country for such data access.  The procedures for such access are provided at:

http://www.census.gov/ces/rdcresearch/index.html

The data used in this project are archived for available use by RDCs.  Two core micro level datasets are used in the
analysis:  jcr_firms.sas7bdat and jcr_estabs.sas7bdat.  They are archived in the directory:  /projects4/arts422/size_age/RESTAT_archive.
There are other derivative datasets (based on these two core datasets) that are also archived there.

When possible we've made public use discloseable versions of the confidential datasets used in the paper available with this packet.
